General

What is the site's mission?

Visit this link to learn more about our site.

How often do surveys appear?

From time to time, we will conduct an occasional survey for guests and members. At most, we conduct it every 6 months.

Are surveys mandatory to answer?

No. You can opt out if you don't want to answer the survey. Although we recommend you to answer it, this is basically a win-win situation for both users and staff as this will help us improve our services to you.

Buying

I'm interested in a certain ad, how do I transact with the seller?

Go to the ad you're interested in. You'll see the seller's provided contact details on how to contact him/her. You can also PM (private message) the seller as well.

I'm interested in a certain ad, but I'm not available for meetup. Are there shipping fees included?

The ad has a section where shipping could be an option. If the seller can have the item shipped, there will be a list of shipping companies that the seller prefers. However, the seller may impose additional fees so you won't just pay for the ad price, but also the shipping fee (should the seller include it).

Are auctions available?

No, the site is designed for a one-way transaction. If there are multiple potential buyers for a certain ad, the seller has the right to pick which among them gets the priority to be contacted first. Unless you're trying to haggle for a lower price, he could just consider you first.

Selling

How do I sell an ad?

Go to User Dashboard -> Ads and look for the button "Sell Ad". It shall take you to the selling ad form and you have to complete the required steps to be able to have your ad undergo an approval phase before getting the ad posted on the site.

Is there a limit to the amount of ads I get to sell on a given time?

Yes. Currently, we allow our members to post an unlimited number of ads, as long as they adhere to our rules.

My item is second hand, is it recommended to sell it the same amount I bought it for?

In some cases, yes. If it is still as pristine as new items, you could sell it for the same amount. However, since the item has been used/utilized, it's better to sell it for a slightly lower price than original.

Using the Site

I am lost and I don't know where each page goes. Where do I go?

Visit the Sitemap page to see the site's structure and where each page goes.

I want to email Game Over staff, but I want to remain anonymous. Do I need to provide my name in the Contact Us page?

No. You can opt not to provide your first and last names. But the e-mail is required so that we can contact you about the status of your inquiry. We keep your information confidential. Read our Privacy Policy page to learn more about how we protect user information.

Premium Membership

How do I acquire a "premium membership"?

Go to User Dashboard -> Premium Membership and pay the required amount of P150 / month.

How long do "premium membership" lasts?

The premium membership is good for 30 days, unless renewed. If the membership is renewed, another 30 days will be added to the user's account.

Can "premium membership" be renewed before it expires?

Yes, and we, the staff, recommend taht you renew your membership before it expires. This avoid conflicts and delays of processing your transaction with the site.
