About

Our one-stop online shop provides a convenient and easy shopping to our customers at user-set prices. Users get to decide on how much they would like to sell their items. There is no limit on how many items each user gets to sell, as long as they're in accordance to the rules. The ability to rate a user after a transaction is present too, so that other users know the reputation of the user on criteria such as quality of items being sold, clarity and uniqueness. All customers are welcomed but users below 18 years old need parental consent to use the site since most items may be too vague for them.

Mission

to offer the best possible user experience in the e-commerce industry through ease of use of the site, promos, friendly staff and the convenience of selling items online (which increases possible buyers to potentially buy their items).

Goals

  1. to satisfy customers with ease.
  2. to provide a one-stop shop to customers for buying and selling.
  3. to provide a convenient shopping destination for all customers.
